🌞 Solar Ops – Solar Plant Monitoring & Operations System
📌 Project Overview

Solar Ops is a full-stack web-based platform designed to monitor, manage, and optimize solar power plant operations. The system provides real-time insights into energy generation, performance metrics, and operational health, enabling efficient decision-making and improved energy output.

It is built to support solar operators, engineers, and administrators by centralizing all plant data into a single, intuitive dashboard.

⚡ Key Features
📊 Real-Time Monitoring
Track live solar plant performance including power output, energy generation, and system status.
📈 Performance Analytics
Visualize historical and weekly energy data to analyze trends and efficiency.
🚨 Alerts & Notifications
Detect system anomalies and trigger alerts based on severity levels.
🏭 Plant & Device Management
Manage multiple solar plants, inverters, and associated components.
👤 User & Role Management
Role-based access control for admins, operators, and viewers.
📅 Reporting Dashboard
Daily, weekly, and monthly energy summaries with KPIs.
